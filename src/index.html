Our Policies

Outline

  • Academic and Career
  • Social and Entertainment
  • Welfare
  • Accountability and Productivity

Academic and Career

  • Help students learn new skills and improve on their existing skills by organizing skill-based workshops.
  • As part of normal schoolwork, students will be enrolled on various online courses to enable them to learn from different perspectives.
  • Ensure that each student joins at most 2 CITSA clubs to improve their technical skills.
  • Set up an Internship committee to help students secure better internships.
  • Establish partnerships between the CITSA clubs and other tech clubs on campus to improve relationship between students and industry players.
  • Make the needed study materials available to students

Social and Entertainment

  • Our social media handles will be readdily available and supportive to any member of the association who has worked on a project and would want to show it to the world
  • Socialisation event for all members every semester.This will help fresh students to meet continuing students and find mentors from amongst them
  • Monthly all clubs meetups with members and facilitators of the various tech clubs on campus.
  • Weekend Aerobics and Fun games every two weeks

Welfare

  • Educate and expose students to available scholarship opportunities to help them fund their education.
  • Provide emotional and mental health support to students by introducing all programme levels to their academic counselors.

Productivity and Accountability

"Numbers don't lie"

  • Clubs will give weekly reports on their progress
  • Citsa Weekly/Monthly report which includes upcoming events, motivational writings, Industry news and available opportunities for students.
  • Data will be gathered and showcased throughout the whole time.
    • E.g., Number of full stack developers
    • Number of members who attended the various club meetings
